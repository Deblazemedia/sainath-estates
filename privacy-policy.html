Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 19/06/2025  |  Website: www.sainathestate.com

1. Introduction

At Sainath Estate, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you may share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website or our services. By accessing or using our site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. No Account Registration

We do not require or allow users to create personal accounts or profiles on our website. You are free to browse our property listings and service information without the need to sign up or log in. This ensures that your interaction with our platform remains anonymous unless you choose to contact us voluntarily.

3. Information We Collect

While we do not collect personal information through accounts or file uploads, we may receive certain information voluntarily submitted through inquiry or contact forms. This typically includes:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Message content or property interest

We also automatically collect non-identifiable data through standard web technologies such as cookies and analytics tools, including browser type, device information, pages visited, and time spent on the site.

4. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide via contact forms is used solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiries, providing property-related services, or sharing information you’ve requested. We do not use your data for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Automatically collected data helps us analyze website traffic, improve user experience, and identify areas of improvement. This data is used in aggregate and is not linked to any specific individual.

5. Confidentiality and Data Protection

Sainath Estate maintains strict confidentiality of all personal information shared with us. We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose your information to third parties, except where legally required. All form submissions are stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel for the purposes of client communication or service delivery.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party services or real estate platforms. Once you leave our site or are redirected to a third-party website, you are no longer governed by our Privacy Policy. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites and encourage you to read their policies before engaging with them.

7. Use of Cookies

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance user experience. Cookies help us understand how visitors use the website and support basic functionality like page navigation and form submission. You can choose to disable cookies via your browser settings, but this may affect the usability of certain features.

8. Data Retention

We retain form submission data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or to comply with applicable laws. You can request that your data be deleted at any time by contacting us using the information provided below.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal information you have submitted to us. You also have the right to withdraw consent at any time. If you wish to exercise these rights, please reach out to us via the contact section below.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for users who are 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect or store information from individuals under the age of 18. If we become aware that information has been submitted by a minor without parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make significant changes, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of the page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes your agreement to the updated policy.
